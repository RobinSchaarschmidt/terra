# 🌍 Terra Protocol

**Constitutional Layer for Planetary Coordination**

---

## What is Terra?

Terra is not a currency, not a government, and not a platform.

Terra is a constitutional system designed to:
- protect planetary commons  
- align economic activity with physical reality  
- enforce long-term responsibility  

It operates as a neutral layer above existing systems.

---

## Why Terra exists

Current systems fail under three conditions:

- Short-term optimization dominates long-term survival  
- Global dependencies are managed locally and inconsistently  
- Trust is delegated, not verified  

Terra introduces a system where:

**Trust is not assumed — it is proven.**

---

## System Structure

Reality → Constitution → Economics → Governance → Verification → Infrastructure

Each layer constrains the next.

## Design Principles

- The Earth is not an asset  
- Commons cannot be owned  
- Decisions must be provable  
- Long-term impact outweighs short-term gain  
- Systems must remain auditable and reversible
