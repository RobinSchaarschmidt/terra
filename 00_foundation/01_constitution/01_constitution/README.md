# Terra Constitution

The Constitution defines the non-negotiable foundation of Terra.

It establishes:
- what cannot be owned
- what must be protected
- what decisions must respect

## Role in Terra

The Constitution sits above:

- economics  
- governance  
- validation  
- infrastructure  

No system component may violate constitutional constraints.

---

## Core Idea

The Constitution does not optimize for efficiency.

It optimizes for:
- long-term survival  
- system integrity  
- irreversible risk prevention
